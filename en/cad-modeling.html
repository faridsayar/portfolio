Typical turnaround: 7–10 business days

What is included

We model parts and assemblies with production logic in mind: wall thickness, draft where relevant, split lines discussed upfront, and exports in the formats your supplier expects. You receive native project files when agreed, plus neutral formats for sharing.

Who this is for

Founders preparing for injection molding, mechanical engineers who need industrial design support, and teams stuck between a pretty render and a part that can actually be made. If you only need marketing images, see our product rendering service.

How it works

Inputs and constraints → CAD build → review package (screenshots + section views) → revision → final export pack. We flag risks early (undercuts, thin walls, ambiguous interfaces) so you do not discover them at tooling.
