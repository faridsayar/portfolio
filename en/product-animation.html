Product Animation Studio for Marketing & Launch

Explain your product in motion before you shoot live video. We prepare an animation-ready 3D setup and deliver a short hero clip for your website, paid ads, Kickstarter page, or sales deck — produced as a studio scope, not an open-ended motion gig.

  • Animation-ready 3D model setup
  • 15–30s product animation (rotation / hero shot)
  • HD MP4 for web, ads, and pitch decks

Typical turnaround: 10–14 business days

What is included

We build or adapt a 3D model for motion, light the product for clarity (not gimmicks), animate a focused camera path or turntable, and export a compressed MP4 ready for web and ad platforms. Loop-friendly versions can be discussed in the brief.

Who this is for

Teams launching hardware on crowdfunding, SaaS-with-hardware brands, and marketers who need a credible product clip before physical samples ship. Need stills instead? Photorealistic product rendering is the better entry point.

How it works

Brief and references → animatic or style frame approval → final animation → MP4 handoff. We keep motion simple and product-forward so load times and ad approvals stay predictable.
